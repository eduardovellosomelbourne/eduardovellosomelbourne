Research collaborations

I am always on the lookout for interesting research problems to solve. I am interested in collaborating with companies of all sizes, government institutions, and NGOs who would like to explore solutions for future-driven research problems. These collaborations can happen in many forms, e.g. research consultancies, developing a system prototype, funding a collaborative PhD student project, professional development, and presentations. Please feel free to drop me a line to discuss how we can work together.

PhD Supervision

If you would like to do a PhD in my team, please do reach out and we can discuss what opportunities are available. To be competitive for a scholarship, you will need an 85% WAM (international applicants) or 80% WAM (Australian citizens and permanent residents) and previous research experience (e.g. having completed an Honours or Master's thesis). I have broad research interests and I am always happy to discuss project ideas with talented applicants.
